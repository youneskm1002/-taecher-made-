Free 3rd Grade Reading Comprehension Worksheet

A simple reading activity with a short passage, comprehension questions, and an answer key for elementary classroom practice.

Reading Resource

Reading comprehension is an important part of elementary learning. This worksheet is designed for 3rd grade students and can be used during reading practice, small group work, independent work, homework, or review time.

The activity includes a short reading passage followed by simple questions that help students practice understanding details, identifying the main idea, and answering in complete sentences.

Teacher Note

This resource is simple and flexible. Teachers can use it as a quick classroom activity, a reading center task, or a short review after a reading lesson.

What Is Included?

This reading comprehension activity includes the main elements teachers usually need for a clear and practical classroom task.

  • A short reading passage suitable for 3rd grade students.
  • Comprehension questions about the text.
  • Vocabulary practice based on the passage.
  • A short written response question.
  • A simple answer key for quick correction.

How Teachers Can Use This Worksheet

  1. Ask students to read the passage silently.
  2. Read the passage again as a class or in small groups.
  3. Ask students to underline important details in the text.
  4. Let students answer the questions independently.
  5. Review the answers together and discuss the main idea.

Suggested Classroom Use

The worksheet works well during a reading lesson, but it can also be used as a warm-up activity, homework task, substitute teacher activity, or reading center resource.

Skills Practiced

  • Understanding a short text.
  • Finding important details.
  • Identifying the main idea.
  • Answering comprehension questions.
  • Writing complete sentence responses.

Tips for Differentiation

Teachers can adapt this activity depending on student level. For students who need support, the passage can be read aloud before they answer the questions. For advanced students, teachers can ask them to write an additional paragraph or create their own questions.

Final Teaching Idea

After completing the worksheet, ask students to share one important detail from the passage. This helps them review the text, practice speaking, and strengthen comprehension through discussion.
